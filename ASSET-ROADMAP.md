# FYNX Asset Generation Roadmap

## Nano Banana Image Prompts

All images should follow the FYNX brand: dark, atmospheric, precise, cinematic. Warm gold/amber accent lighting. Australian context. Premium editorial quality. 2K resolution minimum.

### 1. Hero Background (Homepage)
**Prompt:** Aerial view of a modern prefabricated home being craned onto a rural Australian building site at golden hour. The modular home is suspended by crane cables above a prepared concrete slab. Dramatic warm light. Long shadows. The surrounding landscape is dry Australian eucalyptus scrubland. Shot from 45 degrees above. Cinematic, editorial quality. Dark moody atmosphere with warm gold highlights. No people visible.
**Usage:** Homepage hero background at 5-10% opacity behind text
**Size:** 2560x1440

### 2. Factory Interior
**Prompt:** Interior of a modern prefab home manufacturing factory. Cross-laminated timber (CLT) panels being assembled under precision industrial lighting. Warm amber overhead lights create dramatic shadows. The factory floor is clean and orderly. Robotic assembly arms visible in background. Australian industrial setting. Dark cinematic mood. Shot from eye level looking down the production line. No people.
**Usage:** Prefab Home Finance page, How It Works page
**Size:** 2560x1440

### 3. Modular Home Detail — Crane Installation
**Prompt:** Close-up of a modular home module being lowered by crane onto steel connection points. The focus is on the precision of the connection — steel brackets, bolts, the gap between module and foundation. Golden hour light catching the metal. Australian suburban setting visible in soft background bokeh. Architectural detail shot. Dark and precise.
**Usage:** How It Works milestones section
**Size:** 1920x1080

### 4. Completed Modular Home — Australian Landscape
**Prompt:** A completed modern prefabricated home in an Australian coastal setting. Clean architectural lines, timber and steel facade, large glazed openings. Native landscaping. Late afternoon light creating warm tones on the building surfaces. The home looks premium, contemporary, and distinctly Australian. Wide establishing shot. Cinematic depth of field.
**Usage:** About page, homepage "Who We Help" section
**Size:** 2560x1440

### 5. Modular Townhouse Development
**Prompt:** A row of 6 modern modular townhouses in an Australian suburban context. Consistent architectural language — timber cladding, steel accents, planted setbacks. The development looks cohesive but not monotonous. Late afternoon light. Shot from street level at slight angle. The neighbourhood context is visible — established trees, Australian street furniture. Premium residential.
**Usage:** Developer Finance page
**Size:** 2560x1440

### 6. Blueprint / Technical Drawing
**Prompt:** A large architectural blueprint or technical drawing of a modular home system, spread across a dark timber desk. Gold ambient lighting from above. Pencils, a metal ruler, and a laptop visible at edges. The drawing shows modular assembly sequences — factory, transport, crane, installation. Technical precision. Dark moody overhead shot. No people.
**Usage:** Homepage grid background enhancement, How It Works page
**Size:** 1920x1080

### 7. OG Image / Social Preview
**Prompt:** Dark background (#09090B). The word "FYNX" in clean bold geometric sans-serif typography, centered. Below it, the line "Modern Construction Finance" in smaller, lighter type. A single thin gold horizontal line between the two. Minimal, typographic, architectural. No imagery — pure brand mark treatment.
**Usage:** og-image.png (1200x630)
**Size:** 1200x630

### 8. Abstract Grid Pattern
**Prompt:** Abstract geometric pattern of intersecting gold lines on a near-black background. The lines form a grid that subtly suggests modular assembly — some segments are thicker, some are dashed, some terminate at precise points. The overall composition feels like an architectural plan or circuit diagram. Warm gold (#D4A853) on dark (#09090B). Minimal. Precise.
**Usage:** Decorative backgrounds, section dividers
**Size:** 2560x1440

## Higgsfield Video Prompts

### 1. Hero Background Video — Module Assembly Sequence
**Source image:** Factory Interior (Image #2 above)
**Motion:** Slow dolly forward through the factory floor. CLT panels move along assembly line. Warm amber lights pulse gently. Dust particles float in light beams. 6-10 seconds, seamless loop. Cinematic slow motion.
**Usage:** Homepage hero background (autoplay, loop, muted)

### 2. Crane Installation Timelapse
**Source image:** Crane Installation (Image #3 above)
**Motion:** Slow descent of module onto connection points. Gentle camera vibration on landing. Golden hour light shifts slightly. 4-6 seconds. Dramatic reveal moment.
**Usage:** How It Works page, social media

### 3. Blueprint Lines Drawing Themselves
**Source image:** Blueprint (Image #6 above)
**Motion:** Lines of the technical drawing appear sequentially, as if being drawn in real-time. Grid first, then structural outlines, then detail annotations. Gold lines on dark background. 8-12 seconds. Precision drafting motion.
**Usage:** Homepage background section, loading/transition element

### 4. Aerial Development Pan
**Source image:** Townhouse Development (Image #5 above)
**Motion:** Slow aerial drone pan across the completed development. Golden hour light moves across facades. Subtle life — a light turning on, a tree moving. 6-8 seconds, cinematic.
**Usage:** Developer Finance page hero, social media
